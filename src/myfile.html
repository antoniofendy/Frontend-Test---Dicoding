Welcome to My File Page

Here, you could make a list of your movies.
Below are the steps to configure your movie list!

Add Movie to The List
  • Search or choose the movie in the Home Page
  • Click detail button of the movie
  • Click add button of the selected movie
  • The movie will be listed at your movie list
Remove Movie from The List
  • Choose the movie from My File Page
  • Click detail button of the movie
  • Click remove button of the selected movie
  • The movie will be removed from your movie list

Note :
1. You are only can add movie to the list from Home Page
2. You are only can remove movie from the list from Current Page
